Table tennis

I began playing table tennis in the club when I was about 8 years old. It's a fun sport for me because it requires precision and doesn't require much movement. Because of school and my location, I don't have time to go to the club and play anymore. But even though I don't play as often as I used to, I still love table tennis. It's a great way to improve my hand-eye coordination and it's fun to play with friends or family. I also like that it's a low-impact sport. Overall, it's a great hobby for me. It's fun, challenging, and rewarding.

Programming

I began with programming about 2 years ago, starting with Javascript and developing a discord bot. It was challenging for me because it was my first time ever programming, and I didn't understand the logic of it. I followed tutorials on YouTube and just did what I was told, but I eventually took a break from Javascript.
After a while, I started to understand how programming works and began coding on my own for my bot. I also tried my hand at creating a website. I also used Python to make discord bots, but I didn't enjoy it as much as Javascript. I found Python to be an easy language to understand and use for creating small programs that help automate tasks.
Now, I want to make programming my career and am learning Java, CSS, HTML, and Windows shell in school. Next year, I will also be learning Javascript, and I plan to continue learning and improving my skills to make programming my job.

Playing Computergames

I've been gaming my whole life. My favorite games are Minecraft and Dead by Daylight. I also played some Valorant, but first-person shooter games aren't really my thing. I prefer casual games like Minecraft or games that I can play with friends and have fun, like Dead by Daylight. I also enjoy playing puzzle platform games like Hourglass, Stray, and Portal.
I love the creativity and freedom that games like Minecraft offer. I can build and explore to my heart's content, and there's always something new to discover. Dead by Daylight is also a lot of fun because it's a multiplayer game and I can play with my friends. It's always exciting to try to outsmart the other players and come up with new strategies.
Puzzle platform games like Hourglass, Stray, and Portal are also a lot of fun for me because they challenge my problem-solving skills and keep me on my toes. I enjoy figuring out the solutions to the puzzles and making my way through the levels.
Overall, gaming is a great hobby for me because it allows me to relax and have fun. It's also a great way to socialize with friends and family, and I enjoy the challenge of trying to improve my skills and beat new levels or challenges.
